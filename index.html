Welcome to Yuan Gao's Lab!

The recent research of Dr. GAO Yuan focused on development of computational methods for omics data, including several widely used algorithms in transcriptomics and metagenomics:

1) a de novo algorithm for sensitive and specific detection of circular RNAs, CIRI/CIRI2;

2) an algorithm for detecting internal structure and alternative splicing events for circular RNAs, CIRI-AS;

3) a reference-free algorithm for quantifying and comparing bacterial growth dynamics from multiple metagenomic samples, DEMIC;

4) an algorithm for robust discovery and quantification of transcript isoform from error-prone long-read RNA-seq data, ESPRESSO.

His lab is broadly interested in development and application of novel computational methods combined with latest sequencing technologies to solve various biological problems, which include but are not limited to the following areas.

1) Metagenomics and microbiome;

2) Bulk and single-cell transcriptomics;

3) Nucleic acid modification;

4) Long-read sequencing data processing and analysis.

Developed Tools

  • ESPRESSO

    (error statistics promoted evaluator of splice site options)

    Transcript discovery and quantification from long-read RNA-seq data

    https://github.com/Xinglab/espresso

  • CIRI

    (circular RNA identifier)

    Identification of circular RNAs (circRNAs) from high-throughput RNA-seq data

    http://sourceforge.net/projects/ciri/files/

  • CIRI-AS

    Identification and quantification of internal structure and alterative splicing in circRNAs

    https://sourceforge.net/projects/ciri/files/CIRI-AS

  • SPIRIT

    (splint improved repeat identifier for transcripts)

    Obtaining consensus sequences from long concatemeric cDNA reads with known splint sequences

    https://github.com/Xinglab/SPIRIT/tree/main/SPIRIT

  • DEMIC

    (dynamic estimator of microbial communities)

    Quantification and comparison of baterial growth rates in multiple metagenomic samples

    https://sourceforge.net/projects/demic/files/

  • CIRI2

    Improved identification of circRNAs based on multiple seed matching

    https://sourceforge.net/projects/ciri/files/CIRI2

  • CRISPR-network

    Reconstruction of phage-bacterium interaction based on CRISPR sequence structure

    http://159.226.67.237:8080/lab_update/Newe/tools.jsp
